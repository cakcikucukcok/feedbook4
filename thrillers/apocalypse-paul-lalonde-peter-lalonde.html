The unthinkable has happened. Buttons are pushed, nuclear missiles fill the skies, and the world waits for the end to come. Only Bronson Pearl and Helen Hannah, co-anchors of the World News Network, can reveal the truth. In an incredible sequence of scenes, readers are treated to a firsthand look at what the world might be like in the minutes and hours following the Rapture.
