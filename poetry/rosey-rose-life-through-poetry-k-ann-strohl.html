On October 11th 2003 my niece, Rosemary Joy Rose was killed in a car accident. Her vehicle caught fire and burned. Rosemary's neck was broken, so she died instantly. How do we say goodbye to a love one without the body? I decided to write a poem for my niece, Rosemary. This was my way of saying goodbye. My sister, Kandy asked me to write a life poem for her favorite 19 pictures of Rosemary. I have compiled this book of 21 poems and 19 pictures as a tribute to Rosemary. This book conveys her short lived life story. In conclusion, if you have lost a loved one or friend. I can only hope and pray that when you read one of Rosemary Joy Rose poems, it will bring back a fond memory of your loved one or friend.
