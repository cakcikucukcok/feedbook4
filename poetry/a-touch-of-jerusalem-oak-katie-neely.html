Katie Neely's poetry in "A Touch of Jerusalem Oak" is a collection of timeless pieces of pure, raw, unadulterated emotion. Poetic pieces that will make you laugh, make you hot, and make you cry. With her evident ability to change the meaning of saying 'I Love You'  from poem to poem. Katie Neely is an idealist but yet somehow she truly understands what love, lust, happiness are. Constantly finding her readers applying her poems to their lives, and knowing exactly at that moment the longing to feel that way again.

If you are hiding from yourself, feel with your eyes and not in action. If your reality is mundane and your vision a grand and passionate symphony, she has composed it and is playing it for all. 

Fall in love and be ethereally lost with Katie Neely and her beautiful verses and again and again, only then your path to your passion for the enjoyment and happiness of simple things....... will be a read and a breath away.....
