Everybody has dozed at the wheel, but when you awake you have unwanted company. You have been visited by a former associate of questionable character whose presence could threaten your future. What is the plan to stop him? The lack of an educated younger generation and the incentive to achieve a higher standard of living, the result could be: the elderly fall prey after dark to the young. Just think, after a life of entanglements, you awaken to a surgeon standing over you.
