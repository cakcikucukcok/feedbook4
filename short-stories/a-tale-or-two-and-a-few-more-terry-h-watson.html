A veritable feast of short stories to tempt the palate of the discerning reader, A Tale or Two and a Few More is a captivating collection of tales about humanity in all its guises. Here we have tales of love, loss, hope, compassion and forgiveness.

Catch a glimpse into the private and personal experience of a World War 1 survivor, and share momentarily, his pain as he revisits his past. Admire the work ethic of Matt, a police officer facing his worst nightmare. Rejoice at a family reunion that brought an extra sparkle to Christmas festivities, or laugh at the consequences for Emily, a young lawyer desperate to find love, but jumps to the wrong conclusion when the man of her dreams arrives.

These and many other characters are to be found within the pages of this collection.
