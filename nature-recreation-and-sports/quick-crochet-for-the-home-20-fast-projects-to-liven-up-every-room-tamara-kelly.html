No crocheter's home decor is complete without a little yarny goodness. From the foyer to the kitchen and every room in between, Quick Crochet for the Home has easy and fresh patterns you'll love to stitch for all your living spaces.

Popular crochet designer Tamara Kelly offers 20 projects that not only stitch up in a flash, but also feature the clean lines and contemporary colors of today's modern home.
