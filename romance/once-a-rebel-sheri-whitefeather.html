"What's the point of loving a woman

when she might just cut and run?"

—Ethan Eldridge, veterinarian

It's not easy living down a wild-child reputation. But

Susan Fortune has never done anything the easy way. Who

would have guessed she'd turn out to be a well-respected

San Francisco psychologist with a knack for helping wayward

teens? Not Ethan Eldridge, her secret crush who had lived in

Red Rock, Texas, seventeen years ago.

Now back on the ranch to pay respects to ailing Ryan Fortune,

Susan lets Ethan in on her little secret. However, the rugged

veterinarian has a surprise for her, too. Their longing was

mutual. And how can he say no to a second chance with

Susan? But Ethan's heart is scarred. And if she wants to keep

him, Susan's got to prove that even rebels have to put down

roots sometime.
