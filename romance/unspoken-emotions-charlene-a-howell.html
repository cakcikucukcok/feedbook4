Reunited at their high schools 10-year reunion, Nadia and Jonathan embark upon a seemingly picture-perfect romance. That is, until Marlena steps in. Nadias best friend from college holds a secret so deep, so dark that it may drive a permanent wedge between these two friends. To add to Nadias dismay, Jonathans ex re-emerges to claim what, in her mind, is rightfully hers.

All of the drama and chaos proves to be too much for career-driven Nadia. She, in a fit of rage and to carefully mask her insecurities, ends the relationship.

They can never truly sever all ties due to the one person that connects them eternally and that they both love more than life itself.

Join Nadia and Jonathan as they meet and overcome the tests and trials of a modern-day romance and prove that love truly conquers all.
