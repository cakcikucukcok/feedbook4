The insightful and reflective poems in this book of poetry speak of the harmony of now and eternity and speak of times endless spontaneity.  These meditative poems celebrate the themes of wholeness, well-being  and harmony.  Reading through these meditative poems, immersed in a contemplative peacefulness, you'll experience a quieting symmetry.

In the course of reading through the four sections of Moments: This to the Next (A Point Called Time, Moments To Hours, Clocks and Faces and Time - You and Me) you will experience a relaxing rhythm flowing throughout the expressive poetry. 

To help slow down a fast pace life style these short one page poems offer the peacefulness found in meditative quietness.  This volume of poems makes a wonderful gift for an acquaintance, a friend or a loved one. The volume  is a nice work to have by your favorite chair or your favorite place of reading.

The short insightful poems of Moments: This to the Next offer a meditative window into mindfulness and personal growth.
