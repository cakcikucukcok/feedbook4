Make your Dreams a Reality. Doll

Dreams can come true when you love what you do. In Behind the Chair, author Doll shares what she loves against the backdrop of the life lessons she has learned while working more than twenty-five years behind the chair as a hair stylist.

A world traveler, current producer and host of Dolls Beauty Show, and former model, Doll narrates an array of experiences and shows how she has used Gods gifts in a positive way. Blending personal quotes and inspirational stories with gems of wisdom from Dr. Dorothy Height, Oprah Winfrey, Dr. Martin Luther King and others, Behind the Chair delivers a story of spiritual growth as well as messages of motivation: It is possible to live a joyful, positive life according to Gods plan.

Including photos and offering uplifting and inspiring advice, Behind the Chair seeks to help you find your passion, strive for excellence amidst the challenges, and keep you smiling during your journey to true happiness.
