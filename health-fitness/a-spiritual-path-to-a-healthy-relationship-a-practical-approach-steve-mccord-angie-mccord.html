We all want our relationships to be healthy-but who knows how to make that happen? Husband and wife authors Steven and Angie McCord, a couple in recovery with more than twenty years each show you what works for them.    

Drawing from lessons learned in their own relationships and Steve's eighteen years of experience in clinical practice, the McCords provide wise counsel about what helps and what hurts a relationship.

The Spiritual Path to a Healthy Relationship offers gentle guidance rooted in spiritual principles that will help readers learn effective tools and solutions for achieving the relationships they desire, including:

•Envisioning the qualities you want in a mate and identifying 'must-haves' and 'deal breakers'

•Transitioning from self-reliance to partnership

•Overcoming relationship fears and getting past intimacy roadblocks

•Nurturing positive communication, gratitude, and spiritual connection

•Applying prayer and meditation for a more peaceful relationship
