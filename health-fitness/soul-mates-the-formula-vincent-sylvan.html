Vincent Sylvan has created a formula using astrological influences as a guidepost for finding ones soul mate. This formula gives the readers action steps on how to narrow their search along the way, to identify this mystical person that Vincent believes we all have. After many years of research, his first marriage for seventeen years, not being able to succeed, he experienced divorce and failure.
