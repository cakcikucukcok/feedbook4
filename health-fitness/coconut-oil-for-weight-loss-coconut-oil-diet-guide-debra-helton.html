Coconut oil is one of the most commonly used natural oils in the manufacture of so many kinds of foods, beverages, personal care items, medicines and things that we use around the house. The lowly coconut, which is where coconut oil is derived, is a staple food for generations among Pacific islanders and the coconut tree is also known as the "tree of life" in some civilizations. Aside from being used as an ingredient in most foodstuffs and personal care products; coconut oil is also used for its natural therapeutic properties. Because of the rare composition of coconut oil, it is known to benefit people with skin care problems, heart ailments, digestive problems and even individuals with very low immunity. And probably what most of us don't know is that coconut oil may also be used for weight loss. This book will give you useful information in that regard.
