Ive Learned a Thing or Two: The Little Wisdom Book of Quotes, Quips, and Comments is the kind of book that just asks to have a favorite bookmark tucked into its pages. That way, one can find ones spot and pick up the latest small nugget of wisdom that flows from the gleanings, thoughts, and insights of Peggy Pennington, the octogenarian who created this collection.

Arranged amid generous space like framed pictures on a wall, the brief quotations, wry aphorisms, and astute perceptions in Ive Learned a Thing or Two will entertain, enlighten, and edify the reader who follows the author to hear what she has learned during her decades of life.

Spending some time with the bits of wisdom contained in the pages of Ive Learned a Thing or Two: The Little Wisdom Book of Quotes, Quips, and Comments will help you to gain a wholesome, healthy, and happy attitude toward your own life. This collection affords you the opportunity to take to heart one of the pieces of advice it shares: Look back for the lessons, be present to prepare, and be open to future opportunities.
