WOODCHIPS:  Essential strategies to achieve greater professional, financial and personal success, written and chopped by veteran entertainment executive and attorney Ed Woods, is intended  for individuals from all walks of life, who are determined to find within themselves the wherewithal to  navigate  rough  terrain,  stand  up  to  adverse conditions,  and learn how to use those experiences to rebound and achieve success at the highest levels.  The true recipe for success is the ability to perceive each obstacle as a “deal” that must be negotiated and closed to help improve your overall position.  Ed Woods' message is simple:  Obstacles and challenges can be transformed into achievable goals and lasting success!  Ed did it and so can you! 

WOODCHIPS in comparison to other motivational books stands in a lane by itself. The application of a Woodchip provides a unique approach to encourage a successful path as an entrepreneur by challenging others to apply their personal stories to the specific tip outlined in the book.  As your circumstances change in life, use WOODCHIPS as a reference guide to reinforce your goals to ensure you remain on a successful path.
