You Are Your Brand: Building from the Inside Out is a resource for anyone wanting to get motivated about their future and how that future is cultivated. While many focus on building the exterior of a brand, Your Are Your Brand: Building from the Inside Out takes a turn to focus on what it takes to build the interior of a brand.  The book focuses on ensuring that you take a look at the core of who "you" are by providing personal, professional, and business development strategies. Whether a college student preparing for a career or if you're someone who's working on a personal reinvention, You Are Your Brand: Building From the Inside Out  will help you walk away inspired, informed, and motivated to become intentional about presenting the best possible you to the world because you are your brand!
