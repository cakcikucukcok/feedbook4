Can you make money online without a domain name, website, business plan or a tech person by your side? Do the Hustle Without the Hassle shows you how. You don't even need money, until you make money.

If you are 45 or older and you need more income or you lose your job today, what will you do? Some might spend all night registering for online job boards; others might visit your local employment office as soon as possible. Still others will get caught up in the networking whirlwind where you spend scarce money on lunch with people who can't help. Instead of wasting so much time and energy, why not learn to "do the hustle" and make money on your own. It's easier than you think.

Angela Heath is on a mission to disrupt entrepreneurial training for mature Americans by simplifying 21st century solutions for a changing economy.

In Do the Hustle Without the Hassle: A Quick, Simple, Affordable Way to Make Money at 45+, you'll discover:

  • An easy strategy to return back to the future when everyone sold their talents on the open market.

  • Angela's four options for making money doing what you already know and love.

  • Tools to help you uncover your unique skills and talents.

  • Where to get the support you need either free or inexpensively.

  • Clever ways to persuade your millennial children to help you earn more.
