A thief in search of his destiny.

A man in search of his son.

The Messiah fated to meet them both.

Demons & Thieves is an epic tale of demonic powers seeking to destroy, of love that offers freedom, and of man’s choice toward one or the other. This haunting and captivating novel will inspire you to look past the human condition to the story behind the story.

Master storyteller, Brae Wyckoff, weaves together historical figures, the Gerasene demoniacs and the two thieves on the cross, in a heart-wrenching, action-packed,  supernatural adventure that is steeped in humanity. Wyckoff shows us that there is always more to one’s story than what we see and his deeply human tale gives us a glimpse into that story. Find out what happens in this historical novel, Demons & Thieves.
