Love is a gift most seek as it encourages, emboldens our inner spirit and brings laughter. Sadly we squander that gift with our lies, selfishness and deceitful ways. Harmony is replaced by anger, selfishness and mistrust. Our characters travel such a journey as they amble along the river pathway to the ‘Black Swan’ and its riverside garden. They are joined by others who show them a love that is deeper, beautiful in nature and carries the wounds of life without remorse or seeking revenge. As you journey with our friends can you relate to one or the other? In your heart can you find the love to love the enemy within and around you? Such a love will conquer all and encourage you and the countless others you meet.
