A novel about ordinary people whose lives are touched by an extraordinary God!

Dorothy McGuire is seen as one of the very few people who have

integrity, wisdom and compassion. As she demonstrates a determination

to reach the hearts of each person she encounters, she also exhibits a

resilient strength and assertiveness that confronts and challenges those

whose views are seemingly contrary to all reason.

Alfred Townsend touches a notorious gangland in a renowned no-go

area when he confronts its leader, The Duke, while Ian McPherson has

opportunity to demonstrate his karate skills.

Newcomers, including including Mary Osborne’s ex-husband, Raymond,

and Alfred’s bitter wife, Jennifer Townsend, appear on the scene - each

with their respective problems.
