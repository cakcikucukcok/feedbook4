So if God is your everything and for you He provides all things, wouldn't you want to do anything to please Him. Expose your hidden identities, conquer them based on God's Word, and posess all that God has for you because of His love for you and your love for Him and His Word. It's time NOW, to be who God called and experience abundant living. No more masks or walls, JUST GOD!
