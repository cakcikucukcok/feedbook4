Stepping Into Destiny invites young women to discover who God has created them to be. Author Nicole Davenport relates her own journey as a teenager in finding her identity, a feat that is difficult in a culture that places ones importance on good performance rather than who you are. Nicole also brings stories of young people in the Bible and modern history to life in order to teach Kingdom principles. The teenage years can often be a confusing time as we try to answer that burning question, Who am I? Stepping Into Destiny is engrained with a message of hope for young women as they embark on their own journey of finding their destiny. This book is designed to release you to practice what you learn with Questions for Discussion at the end of each chapter. This feature makes it a great tool for small group settings. You are never too young to discover how special you are to God and the purpose He has for your life! Partner with other young women and learn.
