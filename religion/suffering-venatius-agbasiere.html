FINDING TRUE PEACE.....is a very powerful, inspirational, thought provoking, motivational, uplifting and heartwarming book on how we can derive, acquire and find true peace and joy in the face of lifes struggles, challenges, trials, storms, agonies and hardships.There is always a good and a value in every pain we go through in life, this is what this book is all about. Embracing our suffering, suffering in union with Christ and seeing beyond the present suffering and pain to the future glory and gain therein. Summarily, suffering was described by someone as a washing machine; it spins us, it tosses us around, it twists us but at the end, we come out better, brighter and cleaner.
