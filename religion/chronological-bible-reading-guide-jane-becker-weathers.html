In this guide the events recorded in the Bible are arranged in chronological order to help us better understand how time flows through and joins, or occasionally separates, these sixty-six individual books. When seen in context with the historical, political, and religious settings, we can better understand the different lessons told in different styles and literary forms.

I have grouped these readings by chapter and while this does not provide a perfect chronology (if that is even possible), it comes fairly close and helps retain some of the original authors flow when switching between books. The daily readings provide a guide through the entire Protestant canon in one calendar year, but set your own pace and let your eBook Reader keep track of your progress.

This guide is the end result of a decade of personal research into Biblical history and fine-tuning the daily readings to maintain a consistent reading time.

Invest 10-15 minutes a day to increase your faith and understanding of Scripture!
