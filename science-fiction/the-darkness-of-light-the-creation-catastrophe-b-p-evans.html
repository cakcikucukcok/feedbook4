What if this universe was created by someone? What if this universe wasn't going according to plan? What if the creator was hellbent on wiping out entire races that weren't meant to exist? What if one of those races was the human race? The Darkness of Light: The Creation Catastrophe is the first novel in the six-part sci-fi / mystery Darkness of Light series. The main character is Lola Spears, a young teenager whose home island is the centre of weird goings-on that start happening when alien visitors arrive... Lola immediately becomes curious as to what exactly is happening and why. Her curiosity leads her down a dangerous path, but how will she react when she finds out just why aliens are coming to Earth? Will the aliens' plan be fulfilled? And what exactly is the fate of the human race?
