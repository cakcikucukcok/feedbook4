With time to no end at his disposal-over 60 years repeating endlessly-Louis Sasportas, a man forever obsessed by the Shoah, sets out to avenge the six million Jews that were annihilated along with the progeny that could never come to be. What if Ken Grimwood's unparalleled novel, Replay, turned out to be reality? Having found himself in the predicament of leaping back into his past according to a precise timetable, the main character of Unfinished Business continues the leaps he was thrown into in Forty-Four Forever, struggling to focus his lives on a single purpose while seeking some solace in the company of individuals who had marked our lives, including Elvis Presley, Charlie Chaplin, John Lennon, Philip K. Dick, Carl Sagan, and Douglas Adams.
