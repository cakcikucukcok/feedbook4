When Lainie was diagnosed with Epilepsy at 19 there  were three roads to go down -  avoidance, rebellion  or acceptance.

Lainie decided to travel down all 3 roads to see what  she would discover. 

ELECTRO GIRL invites you to share her portrayal of  a life lived with Epilepsy, from denial to acceptance,  in which she hopes to create more awareness and  inspire others to start thinking for themselves in  relation to managing their health. 

Through her raw and honest story Lainie endeavours to empower people to manage their conditions holistically and not to accept that there is only one road to health. 

We have one life, to rise up and learn more about how our body and brain operate in order to make life exceptional!
