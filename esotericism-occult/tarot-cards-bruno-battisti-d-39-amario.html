This very interesting and fascinating project to couple the symbolic value of tarot cards with closely associated sounds is the brainchild of Maestro Bruno Battista D’Amario, a famous concert guitarist and teacher at the Santa Cecilia State Conservatory of Music in Rome. The 22 figures of the Major Arcana of Tarot Cards are all interrelated, symbolising a moral journey as well as several important key concepts - a journey of “inner discovery” (nosce te ipsum). D’Amario’s unique piece of music is an attempt to articulate the subtle relationship between the cards. Although not commercial in nature, his music was certainly inspired by the occult meaning mysteriously conveyed by this spectacular and ancient book of wisdom. Listening to the guitar and indistinct sounds of the magnetic tape while looking at the cards and reading the short notes written by D’Amario can inspire a positive moment of meditation.
