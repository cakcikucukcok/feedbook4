When God Intervenes is an inspiring story of my faith journey. It tells how an encounter with God at a stressful time of my life led me to open my home to care for people suffering the effects of mental ill health, the success, and the deep passion and concern that I felt for these individuals. I encountered hardships, persecution, negative influences, and challenges by people who tried to discourage me. It tells how it did the opposite and stir up courage in me to go the distance regardless of the obstaclesto successfully build and manage what has now grown to become the life-changing organization called Wayside Community Centre in Londons East End. Read the amazing and inspirational account of perseverance and trust in God even amid the trials and be inspired to take God at his word.
