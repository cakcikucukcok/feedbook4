Have you ever written down something that came to you in a dream?

Reflections and heartfelt observations, both spiritual and natural, are all here. The journey continues, the poetry continues, as does learning and spiritual growth. Hopefully this book will lead you to discover your purpose, guide you toward unconditional love, and help you to find your bliss.
