Have you ever wondered why there is an elephant-shaped building on the beach in Margate? Who built a concrete ship, and why? So join author and photographer Kevin Woyce for a breezy tour of New Jersey's coastal history--from pirates, shipwrecks, and lighthouses to grand hotels, abandoned forts, and today's most popular boardwalks. Illustrated throughout with the author's original black & white photographs, plus historic images.
