The book promises an entertaining read with superb visual treats. With his fantasy taking flight, the then-8-year-old author has dished out three moralised treats not just for the little ones but for anyone young at heart. Sinbad: The Untold Story bespeaks the importance of self-esteem with a wordplay on Sinbads name. Camel In The Jungle is a play with the maxim greed leads to fall by relating it to a childs answer to why camels live in deserts and not in jungles. Princess Fabulina & The Unicorn teaches how determination with the right attitude can lead one to achieve even the impossible, whilst playing on age-long myths about the existence and sighting of unicorns. Instilling values is no childs play, yet this book contains simple stories by a child that can indeed help impart positive values of life to children all around the world.
