Mrs. Spiffy is a cool calico cat who loves poetry, space traveling, her friends, and tuna (not in that particular order), and this is the first volume narrating her interesting and exciting life. On board a vinyl record, she and her friend Marlene the hamster visit Saturn's music festival, where their favorite band, Glacier Birdies, is going to play. Feeling protected by the guards of Light, Mrs. Spiffy and Marlene just want to have lots of fun listening to good songs and blending in with the crowd of music lovers, but little do they know that they're going to be involved in a mystery that is going to make them feel vulnerable, putting their friendship and their beliefs to the test... This is a story about Love for readers of all ages.
