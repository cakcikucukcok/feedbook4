This is the tenth book in the - Lady Shilight Series - On her way to Dovonshar to help overthrow the new tyrannical king, Lady Shilight is abducted. Trapped in a mine with a man who wishes her dead, she manages to escape, with him in full pursuit. In the distant town of Rydolke, she learns the recently defeated monarch, King Kuandel and his family, are prisoners in the local dungeon. Lady Shilight devises a plan to free them. While everyone's thoughts revolve around the king, the queen mourns the loss of her child. Unable to stop his coughing, the boy was taken from her and later pronounced dead - or was he? Is the queen's son alive, or is he a ghost child?
