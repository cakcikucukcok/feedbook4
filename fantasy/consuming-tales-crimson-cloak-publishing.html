Volume Four in the Crimson Cloak Anthologies Series benefits the worthwhile charity, World Child Cancer.  Because cancer is a very scary thing. This is not a children's book, however.  Prepared to be scared!

 

Here are thirteen eerie tales plus a couple of true encounters, to chill the spine on a gloomy evening, or anytime you are looking for a scare. All stories have been gifted to this volume and profits are donated to this wonderful charity, World Child Cancer, who are helping children worldwide to survive this scariest of diseases.

 

With a foreword by author Cynthia MacGregor.  There's even a knitting pattern for a chemo cap for cancer sufferers, for you to make for yourself, family or friends.  If you enjoy scary, spooky, or thriller books, if you enjoy short stories, interesting and quick, then this is the book for you.

 

If you are so inclined and generous, you can make them for nearby hospitals and their patients.

 

Be sure to check out our other Anthologies, each for a worthwhile charity.  Volume One is a Glodwyn's Treasure Chest, a children's book benefiting the World Literacy Foundation.  Volume Two is Steps in Time, adult fiction about time anomalies benefiting Alzheimer's Research UK.  Volume Three is Love Matters, adult fiction about love in some form, benefiting International Children's Heart Foundation.  Volume Five is Santa's Little Helpers, a children's book benefiting Action Against Hunger.
