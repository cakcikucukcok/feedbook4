As you will discover Kate finds a book which begins her journey. You soon realise this book is not normal, it possesses great powers that she does not understand. As she unravels its secrets she finds herself in the past. She is in deep trouble; there is no escape from medieval Knights and Lords. There are battles being fought and lives being lost. 'men screaming and falling to the ground. Blood flying in every direction.' 'He swung his sword heavily at her it came down hard and fast and just missed her head. She swung back at him and her blade ripped through his chain mail just catching his flesh beneath.' The medieval time she has entered is brutal. She must fight to save her life and to find her way home. Along the way she meets many people such as Uncle Toddy and Cecil. Along with these new friends Kate finds her way back, but will she ever leave the past behind her. All is revealed in Epoch.
