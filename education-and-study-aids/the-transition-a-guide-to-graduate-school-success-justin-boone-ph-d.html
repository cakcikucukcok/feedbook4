A survey conducted by the United States Census Bureau in 2013 revealed that young adults who held advanced degrees usually made 23 percent more per year than employees with bachelor degrees. Even new hires typically start at a higher level and earn more money if they have an advanced degree. So why don't more people continue their education? Justin Boone believes that many students simply have too many unanswered questions about continuing their education. He walks you through topics such as: · choosing a university that is credible · getting the funding to continue your education · determining what type of degree to pursue · navigating graduate school success · making the most of your degree Whether you're a current student, recent graduate or seasoned alumni, review your options and make intelligent decisions about graduate school with The Transition.
