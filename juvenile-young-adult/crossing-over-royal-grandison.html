Well hello saints! My Name is Royal Grandison. I truly want to honor God and the precious lamb Jesus Christ, king of all kings, for helping me make this book. On October 10th, 2013, my mother passed away from heart failure. Im not looking for any sympathy. I just want to tell the world what God did for me. After my mom passed away, the pride that I once had was destroying me on the inside. I wanted to cry and talk to people I trusted, but the pride that was in me, wouldnt let me. I was angry and ambitious, with a lot of hurt in me. All I kept on saying every day is, Im going to be successful. I was so determined to keep the promise that I made to my mother. While going through all this I still continued to draw; drawing was and still is my escape route from tough times in my life. One night, I was drawing a piece I named, Who Paved The Way. As I was drawing, I started to think about everything in my life; I could no longer hold the weight I carried. I suddenly began to cry. A few weeks later, I decided to go to church. But at the end of that phase God took me through, I saw his glory unfold. Now Im so in love with Jesus! I have direction and guidance now. Dont get me wrong. Im far from perfect, but Gods mercy is greater than all my laws. His grace, mercy and unconditional love for us all is like no other. How can we not praise our God!?! Yes, He can do it in a twinkling of an eye, but He wants to test our faith in Him. Those who serve the Lord will be challenged by Him some point in their lives. He wants to see how much we love and trust in Him. Will our prayers decrease when we experience suffering or will our prayers increase? You have great purpose, beyond what you know, for both the Kingdom of Heaven and this world!
