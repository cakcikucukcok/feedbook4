Devon is a duck who doesn’t like to stay in line when he is growing up on a small island in a pond.  Instead, he is very curious and likes to explore, so his parents keep looking for him and tell him to get back in line.  But a few days later, he is off exploring. 

Then, one day, he confronts a dog and manages to escape when his parents come looking for him.  So he promises he won’t go off on his own any more.  But will he keep his promise?  Or will something unusual draw him away from the line again?  FOLLOW THE DUCKS tells his story.
