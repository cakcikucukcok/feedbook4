This delightful book celebrates the grandfather and the fun times he shares with his grand children. Usually we hear and read about the special grandmother, this time the spot light is on the grandfather, Pop-Pop. When his grandchildren visits him, he fills each moment of their time together with love, fun and laughter. Pop-Pop plays with them and do fun and exciting activities with them to make them laugh and giggle with happiness. Pop-Pop cherishes every moment he gets to share with them. This beautifully illustrated story of fun times between a grandfather and his grandchildren will put a smile on your face and in your heart.
