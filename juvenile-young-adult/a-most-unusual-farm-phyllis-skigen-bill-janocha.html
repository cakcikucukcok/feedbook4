A Most Unusual Farm is an amusing story about a holy cow named Bess. She was worshipped for many years in India and ends up on a small farm in America. There, she meets up with Chester, a horse of a different color; Gretal, a terrified goose; and a handful of monkeys. Bess introduces them to Brother Bull and to the Good News of Jesus.

Phyllis Skigen, herself a loving and inspired lady, brought the action together in a manner that entertains while it teaches. This story is for ages 5 years to young adult.

The story is brought to life through the inspired illustrations created by Bill Janocha, illustrator and cartoonist for Mort Walker, the creator of Beetle Bailey.
