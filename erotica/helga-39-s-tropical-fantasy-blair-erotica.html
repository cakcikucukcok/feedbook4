"What's the matter, Helga?" She looked up and saw the concern in Jeri's face. She hadn't realized how much her misery showed. Jeri ran the caf and the two women had hit it off when Helga began dropping in for coffee and a chat.

Helga shook her head sadly, relieved to have someone to talk to about her situation. "Back home I work in an office and seldom see the sun.
