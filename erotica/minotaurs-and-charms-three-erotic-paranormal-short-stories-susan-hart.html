The Minotaur Of Crete is about an archaeologist hired by a mysterious Greek billionaire as a spelunker to descend into one of the very deep and dark places on his property. There are mysterious secrets, thousands of years old, buried there as they go along a narrow passageway her companion, Mithros, begins to hum a mysterious song; partly in Greek; partly in Latin, and the rest in an unknown to her language which sounds ancient. Third Time's A Charm, is a wry and often funny and steamy fantasy paranormal short story about a man who is told to clean out the garbage from under his house. He discovers a whole lot of smelly trash bags and one old book of spells. Because he hasn't had a woman in a long time, he decides to conjure up one. The first time it doesn't really work out. The Mummified Monkey's Paw is an occult story about a young man visiting a curio shop and finding a mummified monkey's paw, which creates a change in him that he could never have foreseen.
