His mouth was pulled up in a wry grin, and though I was somewhat frightened by the prospect of an unsavory plan, I suddenly found myself willing to do whatever he asked of me. I was a little ashamed of myself, but I knew that there was no way I could say no to a face as handsome as his.Â 

My eyelashes fluttered, and he chuckled - I must've looked quite silly. After all, I'd gotten this affected by a simple smile. However, I'd like to think that any sensible woman would've done the same in my situation.

"What are you planning?" I asked him. He instantly burst into his characteristic laughter, and I couldn't help but smile along. Heart thudding, I held eye contact with him.

"Was it that obvious?" He grinned, fingers still in my hair, playing with it. I nodded, pressing myself flush against him and staring deep into those bright eyes of his.
